How to play

Add up to four players here, select wether they should be AI-controlled and you're ready to go.

AI

The AI will move on its own (depending on its goals and resources) when you click on the hourglass-button below it's leader image. You will be noticed about custom effects it is not able to comprehend yet, like leader or tech tile effects and will need to resolve those for them. When you've done that, just click on the notification to dismiss it.

New mechanics / symbols

Endgame is now triggered when a player has 8 (or 9 in a 3-player-game) victory points at the end of a round. Then, one final round is played, after which the game ends and endgame effects on cards are triggered.

Tech tiles may now have faction-symbols printed on them. Those are tech tiles that are related to those faction. They can be used to activate a connection of a card by flipping them in addition to their printed effects.

Locations now have give the controlling player resources (on the left side) at their reveal turn. They also give the controlling player a victory point as long as they control the location (similar to the alliance tokens). If a an already occupied location gets taken over by another player, this player has to pay 2 troops from his garrison to take it over.

Dreadnoughts now have 4 combat strength. They always need to rest one round after they were put into a conflict. When a conflict is won, for each involved dreadnought the player can get control of a location of their choice.
