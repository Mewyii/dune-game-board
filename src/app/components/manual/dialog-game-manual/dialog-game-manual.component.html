Endgame is now triggered when a player has 8 (or 9 in a 3-player-game) victory points at the end of a round. Then, one final round is played, after which the game ends and endgame effects on cards are triggered.

Locations now have give the controlling player resources (shown on the left side beside the flag) at their reveal turn. They also give the controlling player a victory point as long as they control the location (similar to the alliance tokens).
A Location can get taken with the -symbol. You can only gain control of locations that have at least one of your agents on them.
Already occupied locations can get conquered by other players, but in order to do so, they need to pay 1 additional troop from their garrison.

Dreadnoughts now have 4 combat strength.
They always need to rest one round after they were put into a conflict.
When a conflict is won, for each involved dreadnought the player receives .

The AI will move on its own (depending on its goals and resources) when you click on the hourglass-button below it's leader image.

It is able to use standard effects like getting rewards, choosing or converting rewards and resolving timing or condition based effects.

It is not able to use text-based effects. For cards, effects that it can't resolve yet will be shown in a blue bar somewhere below the leader image. It is also not able to use text-based leader effects. You will need to keep those in mind for the AI.
